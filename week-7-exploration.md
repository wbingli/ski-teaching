## Destination Options

### Summit West
- **Access**: Separate base area
- **Terrain**: Mix of green and blue runs
- **Vibe**: Often less crowded, more relaxed
- **Good for**: Both groups, scenic variety

### Silver Fir
- **Access**: Chair from Central
- **Terrain**: Blue runs, some gladed areas
- **Vibe**: More natural terrain feel
- **Good for**: Group A primarily; Group B if confident on blues

| Factor | Consider |

### Exploration Activities

**1. Scavenger Hunt (optional)**
- Find 3 different run names
- Ski a run with trees on both sides
- Find the best view
- **Purpose**: Encourages exploration

**2. Follow the Leader Rotation**
- Each student takes a turn leading
- Leader chooses terrain and pace
- **Purpose**: Build independence

**3. Terrain Variety**
- Seek out different snow conditions
- Find a roller or dip
- Try a gentle glade (if available and appropriate)
- **Purpose**: Adaptability practice

**4. Games (if time/interest)**
- Slow race (last one down wins)
- Simon says skiing edition
- Copy the trick (fun, safe moves)
- **Purpose**: Pure fun

## Group B: Beginner (1-3 PM)

### Objectives
- Safely explore new terrain at their level
- Build confidence in new environment
- Celebrate 7 weeks of progress!

### Session Plan

| Time | Activity | Terrain |
|------|----------|---------|
| 1:00-1:15 | Meet, travel to destination | Transit |
| 1:15-2:30 | Exploration (appropriate terrain) | West greens/easy blues |
| 2:30-2:45 | Fun activity | See options |
| 2:45-3:00 | Season celebration | Base area |

### Terrain Choices for Group B

**If going to West:**
- Stick to green runs first
- Progress to easy blue if feeling confident
- Instructor leads, sets comfortable pace

**If going to Silver Fir:**
- Only if group completed Week 6 blue successfully
- Stay on main groomed runs
- Have escape plan (easy way back)

### Fun Activities for Group B

**1. Follow the Instructor**
- Instructor makes interesting (safe) paths
- Students follow the same line
- **Purpose**: Builds terrain awareness

**2. Counting Game**
- Count your turns down the run
- Compare numbers at bottom
- **Purpose**: Keeps focus, adds fun element

**3. Photo Stops**
- Stop at scenic viewpoints
- Take group photos
- **Purpose**: Memory making, breaks for rest

**4. "I Spy" Skiing**
- Call out things to look for while skiing
- "I spy another beginner class!"
- **Purpose**: Keeps eyes up, adds engagement

### Safety Reminders for New Terrain

1. **Stay together** - Don't let anyone get separated
2. **Know the trail map** - Understand where you are
3. **Plan meeting spots** - "If separated, meet here"
4. **Check in frequently** - How is everyone feeling?
5. **It's OK to take it easy** - No pressure to push limits
